About Me

Hello and welcome to my digital reading portfolio. I am Ma. Samantha Elizabeth M. Depamaylo from San Pedro, Laguna. Student at FEU Alabang, I am currently in my first year, pursuing a course of BSITWMA. As a passionate reader from the beautiful Philippines, I enjoy exploring diverse genres and delving into the depths of various literary works. This portfolio encapsulates my reading journey, showcasing my insightful reflections, thoughtful interpretations, and the profound impact literature has had on me. It serves as a testament to my love for literature and my dedication to fostering thoughtful reading and discussion. So, dive in and join me on this literary adventure. Enjoy the journey!.
